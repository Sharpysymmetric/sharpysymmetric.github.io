What is Android?

Android is the most popular mobile operating in the world. Boasting 1 Billion plus users, and a wide range of devices that span every price group and style one can imagine has helped the system grow and take up home in many people's pockets. This huge pool of users also creates a huge pool of potential targets for would be hackers. This larger user base is not the only reason Android is seen as the more vulnerable of the mobile operating systems when compared to its rival Apple and iOS.
